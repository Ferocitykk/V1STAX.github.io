Polybot

Automated prediction market trading bot — momentum signals, paper trading, live PWA dashboard.

Overview

What is Polybot?

Polybot monitors Kalshi prediction markets for momentum signals — price velocity, volume spikes, and spread tightening — and automatically places paper trades on Kalshi's demo environment. A live mobile dashboard tracks open positions and P&L in real time.

Current Phase

Phase 2

Paper Trading

Running on Kalshi's demo environment. Flat $10 stake per signal, max 5 open trades. Accumulating win rate data before going live.

Phase 3

Live Trading

Target: ~1 month of paper data. If results are positive, flip to real money at $1–2 stakes with tighter position limits.

Signal Logic

Price Velocity

Detects rapid directional movement in contract prices as a momentum indicator.

Volume Momentum

Tracks abnormal volume spikes that precede significant price moves.

Spread Tightening

Identifies markets where the bid/ask spread is compressing — a sign of increasing conviction.

Architecture

ComponentPurpose
monitor.pyMain poll loop every 5 minutes — Rich terminal dashboard
kalshi_momentum.pyEvaluates momentum signals against threshold (≥ 0.40)
paper_trading.pyDemo order placement and position management
outcomes.pyPolls every 30 min for market resolution and closes trades
server.pyFastAPI web server on :8080 serving the PWA dashboard
database.pySQLite — signals and markets tables
analyze.pyCLI analytics — run with python analyze.py --days N

Dashboard

Live PWA

Mobile-friendly progressive web app with three tabs: Live signals, Trade history, and Paper P&L chart. Served via a Cloudflare Tunnel from local infrastructure — no cloud hosting required.
