Overview

What is PolyWhales?

PolyWhales connects directly to the Polygon blockchain via Alchemy WebSocket and streams every trade from Polymarket's CTF Exchange contracts in real time — no polling, no delay. It identifies high-performing wallets ("whales") by win rate and volume, then surfaces signals whenever smart money opens a new position in a sports market.

How It Works

Live

On-Chain Event Streaming

Subscribes to OrderFilled events on both Polymarket CTF Exchange contracts via Alchemy WebSocket. Every trade hits the system the moment it's confirmed on Polygon.

Live

Token Cache

Maps 100k+ Polymarket outcome token IDs to human-readable market titles and outcomes. Refreshed every 10 minutes from the Polymarket API.

Live

Wallet Scoring

Every wallet that trades in sports markets gets scored by win rate, total volume, and recency. Top 50 wallets are flagged as tracked whales.

Live

Signal Generation

When a tracked whale opens a new position, a signal fires immediately. Signals include the market, direction, size, wallet score, and win rate.

Dashboard

Live Web UI

Four-tab dashboard accessible at whales.ferocity.trade — Live Feed of whale trades, Signal log, Whale leaderboard ranked by score, and Markets overview showing where whales are positioned.
